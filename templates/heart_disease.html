This website was made using a machine learning model to predict your heart health based on your answers to the questions on the form!

We used over 300k records from a 2020 survey regarding heart health to train and test our model.

We tested 8 different models and chose to use a Bi-Directional LSTM for this website as that model gave use the best accuracy of 76%

All you need to do is to answer the questions on the form and press submit!

It will process your answers and give you a probability of how likely you are to have a heart disease!
